Guess The Number

Between 0-100






Instructions

The system selects a random number within a fixed range (example: 1 to 100).
Your goal is to guess the correct number in as few tries as possible.
After each guess, you will receive a hint:
“Too high” → your guess is above the number
“Too low” → your guess is below the number
Keep guessing until you find the correct number.
The game ends when you guess correctly.
Your total number of attempts will be displayed.
Invalid inputs (letters, symbols, empty input) will not count as attempts.
